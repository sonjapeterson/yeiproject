Why BookBase?

Skip the middle man

With thousands of books published every year, and bookstore shelf space shrinking, authors and publishers need a new way to sell books that doesn't rely on brick-and-mortar stores.

Save time

Our website generator and social media tools are customized for authors, so you can build an online presence fast and focus on your real job: writing great books.

A platform for everyone

Even authors published by large publishing companies are left on their own when it comes to online marketing. Our tools work great for both self-published and traditionally published authors.

How It Works

  • Use our Online Author Tour tool to do readings on Skype, hold a contest for signed copies, and answer questions in a fan forum.
  • Spark interest by providing sample chapters that readers can easily share with friends online.
  • Add an email address collector to your site to start using email marketing campaigns.
  • Link to or incorporate content from all your social media accounts including blogs, Facebook and Twitter
